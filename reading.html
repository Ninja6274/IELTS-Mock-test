The History of Chocolate
Chocolate has been enjoyed for centuries, originating from the ancient Mayans and Aztecs. They used cacao beans to create a bitter drink that was believed to have energizing properties. Over time, chocolate spread to Europe, where sugar was added to create the sweet treat we know today. The Industrial Revolution allowed mass production, making chocolate widely accessible. Today, major brands like Cadbury, Hershey, and Lindt dominate the chocolate industry with an ever-growing variety of flavors and forms.
